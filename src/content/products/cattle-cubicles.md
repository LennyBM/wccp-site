## Built for the West Country dairy

Cubicles are the bit of the shed cows lie in for fourteen hours a day. Get them wrong and you'll see lameness, mastitis and a drop in yield. Get them right and they'll outlast three roof recovers.

We've been casting cubicle dividers and kerbs at the Holsworthy yard for over thirty years. Every component is C40/50 grade, vibration-cast, and wet-cured for a minimum of seven days before it leaves the yard.

## Sized for the breed

We hold three lying-length variants in stock — Holstein at 2.4m, Jersey at 2.2m, and a wider 2.6m beef section for sucklers and continental crosses. Mixed herds typically run on the Holstein size with a longer brisket board for the Jerseys.

## Compliant by design

All cubicle components are manufactured to BS EN 14992 (precast concrete products – wall elements) and use BS 8500-1:2023-compliant mixes. Exposure class XC4 as standard, with XA2 sulphate-resisting blends available for slurry-adjacent runs.

## Delivered, set out, ready to fix

We deliver on our own HIAB-equipped flatbeds across Devon, Cornwall, Somerset and Dorset. Larger jobs come with a setting-out drawing and, increasingly, a WCCP install crew.
