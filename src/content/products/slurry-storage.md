## Twenty-year design life, in writing

Slurry is aggressive. The hydrogen sulphide, the sulphates, the cyclic wet-dry — they eat regular concrete. We mix every slurry panel with sulphate-resisting cement (CEM III/A or CEM IV/B) and a low water/cement ratio for low permeability.

The result is a panel rated for a 20-year design life with no maintenance beyond annual visual inspection. We supply the SSAFO 2018 certificate, the BS 8500-1:2023 mix design, and the capacity calc with every quote.

## Three heights, one base

We hold 3.0m, 3.6m and 4.2m heights in stock. Anything taller is bespoke and goes through our engineers — typically with a thicker 280mm wall and a stiffening column at every joint.

## Grant-ready paperwork

If you're applying for the Slurry Infrastructure Grant (SIG) Round 4, we format the quote with itemised capacity per cow, total m³ stored, and a Defra-friendly summary page. Mention the grant on the form and we'll do it as standard.

## Delivered and pitched

For South West installs we deliver on our own HIAB fleet and can pitch the panels into the channel ourselves. Nationwide, we use a vetted haulier network and supply a method statement for your groundworker to set them.
