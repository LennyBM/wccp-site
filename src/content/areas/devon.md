## Devon — our home patch

We've been casting concrete in North Devon for over 55 years. The Holsworthy yard sits ten miles from the Cornwall border and an hour from Exeter, which makes Devon our densest delivery and install territory.

If you're farming in Devon, you've probably driven past one of our slurry stores already. We've cast for over 200 dairy and beef holdings across the county, from the Hartland coast through to the Blackdown Hills.

## What we do across Devon

The bulk of our Devon work is dairy infrastructure — slurry storage, cubicles, feed troughs, parlour and robot bases. North Devon's soft pasture and high rainfall mean SSAFO-compliant slurry capacity is non-negotiable, and we've supplied dozens of SIG-funded stores in the last five years alone.

Civils work tends to cluster around the A30 corridor and the Exeter-Plymouth road. We've done retaining walls for highway widening jobs, padstones for housing developments, and bespoke precast for estate restoration.

## Delivery and install

We deliver across Devon on our own HIAB-equipped flatbeds, usually next-day for stock items and within one delivery slot of pour for bespoke. Our install crews work out of the Holsworthy yard and can be on site anywhere in Devon within an hour or two.

For South Devon jobs (Plymouth, Totnes, the South Hams) we'll usually run a two-load day rather than push a single trip — kinder on the lanes and faster end-to-end.
